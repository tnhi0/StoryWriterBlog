Timeline

Because I tend to do long, drawn-out work, it usually takes about 1 month roughly.

This is what I've done with my previous project and it worked pretty well:

Phase Details
Initial Agreement
  • Set the project budget: How much can you spend on this project?
  • Set the project timeline: When is the deadline? Is it a hard or soft deadline?
First Meeting
  • Tell me the story that you want to tell
    • Who are the characters in your story? What are they like?
    • What is the main feeling that you want to convey?
    • Why do you want to tell this story? Is this something that you have thought of for a while?
    • What is the vibe that you want the story to give off? (chill, formal, wholesome, blue... you can describe it anyway you want)
    • What music do you want the story to be like? I think music is a very good way to specify complex feelings. If there are any songs like that for you, please tell me!
  • We would sort out these things as well:
    • Where will we communicate? When and how often do we do revisions?
    • How and when will the project be financed? (Usually 2 times: at project start and end)
    • When is the deadline? How long will the project last?
    • Are there any other materials necessary for the project? (photos...)
Project Timeline Then I will create a timeline of the project and send it to you. Each week, I'll update the progress of the project and we will meet once a week for revision. Then we wrap things up.
